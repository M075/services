Our Basic package is hosted on a .co.za domain e.g. www.modestdesigns.co.za. Your taylor-made site is hosted on servers with SSD drives which ensures page load times are minimal. The Standard package features additional domain registration options, .co.za is affordable, popular and our recommended choice.
