Modest Designs

Last updated: August 1, 2023

Thank you for choosing Modest Designs for your website design needs. We are committed to providing you with high-quality services and ensuring a smooth delivery process. This Delivery Policy outlines the terms and conditions related to the delivery of our website design services.

1. Delivery Timeline

The delivery timeline for your website design project will be communicated to you at the beginning of the project. The estimated completion date will depend on the complexity of the project and the scope of work agreed upon. We will make every effort to complete the project within the agreed timeframe.

2. Communication and Feedback

During the website design process, we will maintain open communication with you. You will have the opportunity to provide feedback and revisions to ensure that the final product meets your expectations. Timely responses from both parties will help expedite the delivery process.

3. Delays

In the event that unforeseen circumstances or technical challenges arise during the project, there may be delays in the delivery timeline. If such delays occur, we will inform you promptly and work to resolve the issues as quickly as possible.

4. Delivery Completion

Once your website design project is completed, we will provide you with the final deliverables. These may include the website files, design assets, and any additional materials specified in your project agreement.

5. Acceptance of Delivery

Upon delivery of the final project, you will have a specified period of seven business days to review and provide acceptance. If you have any concerns or require revisions, please inform us during this review period. After the acceptance period expires, the project will be considered complete.

6. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our Delivery Policy, please contact us at info@modestdesigns.co.za.
