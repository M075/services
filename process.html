Start the process

STEP 1

Contact us 

Email us or WhatsApp regarding any unsurities or when you are comfortable and ready to go ahead with your website. We will try our best to respond swiftly. Be sure to check out our FAQ's section too. 

STEP 2

Drawing board

We respond to your correspondence answering any queries or concerns and we start the design & planning process. We will be in often contact with you requesting further details or to clarify any unsurities about your business or services offered.

STEP 3

Themes & Fonts

You may suggest the color palette and fonts used for your site or if there's still doubt we can assist with suggestions. Sample templating are displayed on our templates page so you have an idea on the outcome of your website. Should you wish to proceed, payment for the first three months will be set up via debit order.

STEP 4

Testing

Your website draft will be sent to you for review. At this stage you may review your site according to the package you have chosen, changes will be made accordingly.
If all is well, payment of three months will have to reflect in our account before complete delivery takes place. All domains will only be registered thereafter.

STEP 5

Go Live!

Your website will be live for everyone to see. Your domain will either be open-sourced, .co.za or custom, package dependent. Hope you like and market!
