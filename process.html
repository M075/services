Start the process

STEP 1

Contact us via email

Email us regarding any unsurities or when you are comfortable and ready to go ahead with your website. We will try our best to respond swiftly. Be sure to check out our FAQ's section also. 

STEP 2

Drawing board

We respond to your correspondence answering any queries and we start the design & planning process. We will be in contact with you requesting further details about your business or services offered.

STEP 3

Themes & Fonts

You may suggest the color palette and fonts used for your site or if there's still doubt we can assist with suggestions. Sample templating will be sent through so you have an idea on the outcome of your website. Should you wish to proceed, payment for the first month will be set up via debit order.

STEP 4

Testing

Your website draft will be sent to you for review. At this stage you may review your site according to the package you have chosen, changes will be made accordingly. 

STEP 5

Go Live!

Your website will be live for everyone to see. Your domain will either be open-sourced, .co.za or custom, package dependent. Hope you like and market!
